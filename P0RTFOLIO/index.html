Portfolio


Project 1


Super Plumber Bros" is a classic 2D side-scrolling platformer game inspired by the popular game "Super Mario". In this game, players control a skilled frog character who embarks on an exciting adventure. Players must navigate through challenging levels filled with obstacles, collect power-ups, and defeat enemies such as Goombas, Koopas, and other creatures using the plumber's wrench or by jumping on them. The game includes a variety of power-ups, such as mushrooms that grant additional health or size, fire flowers that allow the plumber to shoot fireballs, and star power-ups that provide temporary invincibility.

Project 2


RoboRunner: Master of the City" is a thrilling 3D parkour game where you take on the role of a highly advanced robot with unparalleled agility and speed. Navigate through a futuristic cityscape, overcoming challenging obstacle courses with acrobatic moves such as wall-running and double jumps. Unlock new abilities and upgrades as you progress, and test your reflexes and decision-making skills to become the ultimate parkour master. Experience high-speed action, breathtaking jumps, and epic parkour moves in this futuristic adventure!
